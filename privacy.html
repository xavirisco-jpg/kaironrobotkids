Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Kairon Robot Kids is a family-friendly website. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

Contact messages

The contact form opens your own email application. This website does not store form details in a database. Any message you choose to send is handled by your email provider and the Kairon Robot Kids mailbox.

Children’s privacy

Children should ask a parent or guardian before sending a message or sharing personal information online. We do not knowingly request sensitive personal information from children.

Cookies and analytics

This starter website does not use analytics or advertising cookies. If analytics, embedded videos, or other third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated before those tools go live.

Contact

For questions about privacy, use the contact form on the homepage.
